<objective>
Establish the build-time data layer for the four covered Bundesländer's PLZ → Stimmkreis mappings (NRW, Bayern, Baden-Württemberg, Niedersachsen) and the universal PLZ → Bundesland enrichment via OpenPLZ API.

Purpose: Sub-phase A requires the level router to know (a) which Bundesland a PLZ is in, and (b) which Landtag-Stimmkreis(e) a PLZ belongs to (for the four covered Länder). Without this layer, PLAN 02 cannot match Landtag mandates to PLZs, and PLAN 04 cannot decide whether to fall back to Bund.

Output: Five JSON files plus an `npm run` script. No runtime dependencies, no runtime fetches.
</objective>
