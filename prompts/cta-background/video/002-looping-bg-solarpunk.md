## Prompt

```
A slow, dreamlike aerial flyover of a solarpunk German city at golden hour.
Camera drifts gently forward over green rooftops covered in wildflowers and

The mood is quietly hopeful, like the first warm day of spring. Color palette:
warm creams, soft forest greens, golden light, subtle touches of red (a mailbox,
a door, a flower). No people in sharp focus — life is present but gentle,
ambient. The feeling: "this is what happens when people care."

Style: cinematic, slightly stylized — not hyperreal, more like a Wes Anderson
establishing shot meets a Studio Ghibli landscape. Seamless loop, 10-15 seconds,
16:9, slow and meditative. No text, no UI elements.
```
